Idlescape augement calculator

To use this, check your probability of augmenting an item to +1 (your base chance), and put it in the first field. You can currenly use this calculator for two purposes: finding out the expected costs of augmenting an items to a given level (in the upper part), or finding out how much xp you can expect to get from augmenting - either from scratch of from buying an already augmented item (in the lower part.). To update the output, press enter or deselect a field.

Limitations:

The chances enchantment is not currently supported.
Xp calculations do not take into account leveling up while augmenting, which slightly increases success chances.
Serious floating point errors happen if you try base chances above 99.9, it's not very reliable there. Works fine for actually achievable values though.
Remember that in practise you'll usually get less than average xp; this is offset by gaining a lot on rare, high-level augments.
Also note that you'll need to apply any fish or other xp buffs yourself.
