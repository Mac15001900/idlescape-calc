Idlescape augement calculator

To begin, find your probability of augmenting an item to +1 (your base chance). You can currenly use this calculator for two purposes: finding out the expected costs of augmenting an items to a given level (in the upper part), or finding out how much xp you can expect to get from augmenting (in the lower part.).

Limitations:

The chances enchantment is not currently supported.
Xp calculations do not take into account leveling up while augmenting, which slightly increases success chances.
Floating point errors happen if you try base chances above 99.9, it's not very reliable there. Still works fine for actually achievable values.
